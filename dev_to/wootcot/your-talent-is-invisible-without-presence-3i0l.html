Your talent is invisible without presence.

#buildinpublic #career #discuss #marketing

You can build the most secure architecture in the world.

You can write the cleanest code in the industry.

You can solve the most complex B2B supply chain logic.

But if you aren't present where your customers are, you don’t exist.

In 2026, "Presence" isn't just showing up; it’s:

  • Building in public.
  • Owning your data and your narrative.
  • Being the signal in a world full of AI-generated noise.

Presence is the bridge between a "great idea" and a "successful business".

Do you prioritize building the product or building the presence?
Let’s talk below. 👇

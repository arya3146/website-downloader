What does a request to "Delete My Personal Data" entail?

Requesting the deletion of your personal data means that all your personal information, including your viewing history, videos, playlists, stream posts and direct messages, will be lost. Your profile will not be visible to others anymore and any subscriptions you may have made, will also be lost. You will not be able to reactivate your account or retrieve anything you've added and/or purchased.

Please follow the steps below to request the deletion of your personal data:

  • Enter your email address in the box below and click Submit.
  • Click on the authorization link you will receive via email to confirm your email address.
  • Select the account to which your Data Deletion request pertains.
